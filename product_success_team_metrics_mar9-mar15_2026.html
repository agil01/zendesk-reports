1. Management Summary

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of 72 Zendesk support tickets received during the 7-day period from March 9 through March 15, 2026. The analysis reveals concerning backlog accumulation with only a 58.3% resolution rate for assigned tickets, significant workload imbalance, and critical performance gaps requiring immediate intervention.

⚠️ CRITICAL FINDING: Ron Pineda's 88% open ticket rate (22 of 25 tickets) represents a severe backlog crisis with multiple tickets dating back to July-August 2025. This performance gap is compounded by handling 40% of all urgent tickets (10 of 25 tickets), creating an unsustainable workload that requires immediate managerial intervention and resource reallocation.

Key Takeaways

  • Volume Performance: 72 total tickets (65 assigned + 7 unassigned) with 58.3% resolution rate (38 of 65 assigned tickets resolved)
  • Critical Backlog Crisis: 43 open tickets across team with Ron Pineda accounting for 22 (51% of all open tickets)
  • Performance Disparity: Candice Brown leads with 45% open rate, while Ron Pineda's 88% open rate is nearly double the team average of 66%
  • Workload Imbalance: Ron handles 34.7% of volume (25 tickets) including 40% of all urgent tickets (10 urgent), compared to 27.8% each for Bola and Candice (20 tickets each)
  • Primary Issue Categories: User management (UMAC) 33.8%, Terminations 23.1%, EHR/Athena 21.5%, Application Issues 12.3%
  • Historical Backlog: Multiple tickets dating back 6-8 months indicate systemic closure issues requiring process intervention

6. Appendix: Methodology & Data Sources

Data Collection

  • Source: Zendesk MCP Integration
  • Query: created>2026-03-08 created<2026-03-16 type:ticket
  • Total Records: 72 tickets (65 assigned + 7 unassigned)
  • Date Range: March 9, 2026 - March 15, 2026 (7 days)
  • Collection Date: March 16, 2026

Analysis Methodology

  • Resolution Rate Calculation: (Solved + Closed tickets) / Total assigned tickets × 100 = (24 + 18) / 65 = 58.3%
  • Open Rate Calculation: (Pending + Hold tickets) / Total assigned tickets × 100 = (15 + 8) / 65 = 35.4%
  • Historical Open Tickets: Retrieved using query assignee:[AGENT_ID] -status:solved -status:closed without date restrictions to capture all unclosed tickets across entire history
  • Issue Categorization: Manual classification based on ticket subject, tags, and content analysis across 5 primary categories: UMAC, Terminations, EHR/Athena, Application Issues, Other
  • Priority Distribution: Based on Zendesk priority field values (urgent, high, normal, low)

Key Definitions

  • Resolved Tickets: Tickets with status "solved" or "closed"
  • Open Tickets: Tickets with status "pending" or "hold" (including all historical unclosed tickets)
  • Open Rate: Percentage of agent's assigned tickets that remain unclosed = Open Tickets / Total Assigned Tickets × 100
  • Backlog: Total count of open tickets across all agents and time periods, representing accumulated unclosed work
  • Historical Tickets: Open tickets created before the current reporting period but still unclosed, indicating chronic backlog issues
  • UMAC: User Management and Admin Console—tickets related to access, permissions, role updates, and user lifecycle management
  • EHR/Athena: Electronic Health Record and Athena integration—tickets related to clinical application functionality, connectivity, and integration issues
  • Terminations: User account deactivation and offboarding requests
  • Application Issues: Software bugs, performance problems, and technical defects in platform functionality
